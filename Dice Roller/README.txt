/r/dailyprogrammer #364

Uses user input or the challenge.txt to create a diceroll and then print the results.



Input:
	NdM, where N is the amount of dices to be rolled and M is the amount of sides the dice has.

	Example:
	3d6 - 3 dices, 6 sides (traditional dice)
	4d12 - 4 dices, 12 sides
	1d10 - 1 dice, 10 sides
	5d4 - 5 dices, 4 sides


Output:
	<Dice>: [N1, N2, N..., Nn] - <SUM N>

	Example:
	3d6: [3, 4, 6] - 13
	4d12: [2, 10, 3, 11] - 26
	1d10: [5] - 5
	5d4: [3, 3, 2, 4, 1] - 13
